Collective Tower

Tripoli, Lebanon
A project by Adrian Lahoud with Alina McConnochie, Sally Hsu, Regan Ching & Erik Escalante

The project addresses the co-existence between different city forms, specifically the small-scaled, fine-grained building divisions within towers and skyscrapers Like many cities in the Middle East, Tripoli is an old and finely-grained city with small scale forms that have been developed by tradition and necessity over centuries. With the recent advent of technology and globalism these details are being replaced with generic forms of high-density development. Adrian Lahoudâ€™s proposal explores this problem with a tower that has a reduced footprint that minimally disrupts the fabric of the existing city.
